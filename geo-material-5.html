The Hydrosphere

The total system of water on Earth, encompassing all rivers, vast oceans, underground tables, and solid frozen glaciers.

1. Water Distribution

Saltwater: Makes up roughly 97% of all water on Earth, found inside global oceans.

Freshwater Storage: Out of the remaining 3%, the vast majority is trapped as solid ice inside glaciers and polar ice caps.

2. Rivers & Water Dynamics

Longest River: The Nile River in Africa. (Note: The Amazon holds the largest volume of water flow).

Basin: The full geographical area of land that naturally drains water down into a specific river network.

Tsunami: An immense, destructive ocean wave triggered by sudden underwater tectonic earthquakes.
